RUMORS & LEGENDS

In the world of pirates and leaked video games, we rarely know the exact truth. We use the Passive Voice with Reporting Verbs to distance ourselves from the information.

It is said / believed / thought that...
Active: People say that Black Flag is the best game.
It is said that Black Flag is the best game.
Active: Journalists report that a remake is coming.
It is reported that a remake is coming.

THEORY OF RUMORS

Structure 1: Impersonal Subject (It)

Click the words to change the rumor.

ADVANCED THEORY

Structure 2: Personal Subject + To Be/To Have

You can start the sentence with the subject itself.
Use to be for present facts, and to have + participle for past actions.

The remake
is
expected
to be delayed until 2026.
